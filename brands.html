Our brands

Part Two

Part Two was established in Copenhagen, Denmark in 1986.

With more than 30 years experience they produce high quality, timeless yet modern clothes that seem to be a feature of Danish design.

The superior fabrics they use carry a slightly higher price tag but are worth every penny.

ICHI

Founded in 1999 and part of the Danish fashion mogul DK Company - ICHI has delivered high quality pieces in an accessible price range for almost two decades.

Mistral

Started by father and daughter, John and Tracey - the first Mistral shop opened in Malmesbury in the mid 1990s.

The Mistral brand is known for its gorgeous colourful prints, easy to wear but flattering designs and fantastic value for money.

Thought

A natural clothing company based in London.

Their philosophy is to design and make beautiful, timeless fashion while caring for the environment.

They have been championing sustainable eco fibres such as hemp, bamboo and organic cotton since the mid 1990s - these fabrics are now readily available in many other brands.
