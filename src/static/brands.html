Our brands

Mistral

A brand started in 2008 by father and daughter duo John and Tracey.

Their products are designed in-house, with prints inspired by the country, town and sea. Versatility, attention to detail and a carefully selected colour palette make Mistral a key brand for women.

Braintree

A natural clothing company based in London.

Their philosophy is to design and make beautiful, timeless fashion while caring for the environment. They have been championing sustainable eco fibres such as hemp, bamboo and organic cotton since the mid 1990s. Their quality affordable garments are always produced ethically.

Saskia

A design house producing a selection of separates from high quality jersey, silk velvet and chiffon in flattering shapes, ideal for the party season!

Ochre

They design beautiful ranges of one size coloured knitwear in cashmere and cotton blends.
